Thailand Trip - Day 4

Welcome to Day 4 of our Thailand adventure!

Activities

  • Visit the Grand Palace
  • Explore Wat Pho and see the Reclining Buddha
  • Enjoy a traditional Thai lunch
  • Take a boat ride along the Chao Phraya River
  • Visit the local markets

Tips

Make sure to dress appropriately when visiting temples. Stay hydrated and enjoy the local cuisine!
