Thailand Trip - Day 1

April 24th

Welcome to Day 1 of our Thailand trip! Today, we will explore the vibrant city of Bangkok.

Activities:

  • Visit the Grand Palace
  • Explore Wat Pho and see the Reclining Buddha
  • Enjoy a traditional Thai lunch
  • Take a boat ride along the Chao Phraya River
  • Visit local markets for shopping

Tips:

Make sure to dress appropriately for temple visits and stay hydrated!
